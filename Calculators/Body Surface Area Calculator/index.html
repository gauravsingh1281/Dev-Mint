About BSA Calculator

What is BSA?

Body Surface Area (BSA) is a calculated measure of the total surface area of the human body. It's widely used in medicine for various purposes including drug dosing, metabolic rate calculations, and cardiac output measurements.

Formulas Used

  • Du Bois Formula: BSA = 0.007184 × W^0.425 × H^0.725
  • Mosteller Formula: BSA = √((W × H)/3600)
  • Haycock Formula: BSA = 0.024265 × W^0.5378 × H^0.3964

Applications

  • Drug Dosing
  • Metabolic Rate Calculations
  • Cardiac Output Measurements
  • Burns Assessment
